# Transition Events

Transition events occur between two events of the same case ID and bridge the gap between events that occur at process
steps.
They are depicted as small circles on a transition path. These circles serve as visual cues, shedding light on the
degree of utilization or efficiency of these transitions, encapsulating the extent of their utilization.

The circles are customizable in terms of color and size. For instance, events sharing the same case ID can be unified
with identical colors and sizes, facilitating the easy tracking of specific cases throughout the entire process.

By default, the <TypeLink type="ProcessMining" /> component provides bluish transition events which can be disabled.
